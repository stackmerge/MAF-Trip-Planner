# Hotel Booking Policies — Global

---

## Check-in / Check-out

### India
- Standard check-in: 2:00 PM | Check-out: 12:00 PM
- Budget hotels (OYO, FabHotels): Check-in 12:00 PM | Check-out 11:00 AM
- Early check-in (before 8 AM): Full extra night charge
- Early check-in (8 AM–2 PM): 50% of one night's rent
- Late check-out (12 PM–6 PM): 50% of one night's rent
- Late check-out (after 6 PM): Full extra night charge

### USA
- Standard check-in: 3:00–4:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM–12:00 PM
- Early check-in: Subject to availability; fee $25–$75 or free for loyalty elite members
- Late check-out: Subject to availability; fee $30–$100 or free/extended for elite members
- Extended stay brands (Residence Inn, Homewood Suites): check-in from 3:00 PM

### Europe
- Standard check-in: 2:00–3:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM–12:00 PM
- Budget (Ibis, B&B Hotels): Check-in 2:00 PM | Check-out 12:00 PM
- Luxury: Check-in from 3:00 PM; late check-out often complimentary for suites/top tiers
- Early check-in fee: €20–€60 or subject to availability

### Middle East
- Standard check-in: 3:00 PM | Check-out: 12:00 PM
- Luxury properties (UAE, Qatar): Flexible for suite guests; butler-arranged
- Ramadan hours: Restaurants and some facilities may have modified hours

### Asia-Pacific
- Standard check-in: 2:00–3:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM–12:00 PM
- Japan: Strictly 3:00 PM check-in; early arrival bags stored; late check-out usually charged
- Southeast Asia budget hotels: Often 12:00 PM check-in / 11:00 AM check-out

### India
| Policy Type         | Window                   | Charge                          |
|---------------------|---------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Free Cancellation   | Up to 48–72 hrs before   | No charge, full refund          |
| Partial Cancellation| 24–48 hrs before          | 1 night's rent charged          |
| Non-refundable      | Any time                  | No refund; credit may apply     |
| No-show             | —                         | Full booking amount forfeited   |

Refund timeline: 5–7 business days (UPI/Debit), 7–10 days (Credit Card)

### USA
| Policy Type         | Window                   | Charge                          |
|---------------------|---------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Free Cancellation   | Up to 24–48 hrs before   | No charge, full refund          |
| Non-refundable      | Any time after booking    | No refund                       |
| Resort fee          | Charged regardless of cancellation | $20–$60/night mandatory |
| No-show             | —                         | Full first night + taxes forfeited |

- Major OTA bookings (Expedia, Booking.com): Cancellation terms set by individual property
- Loyalty members: Extended cancellation windows at some brands (Marriott Bonvoy Platinum: 48 hrs)

### Europe
| Policy Type         | Window                   | Charge                          |
|---------------------|---------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Free Cancellation   | Up to 24–72 hrs before   | No charge, full refund          |
| Semi-flexible       | 24–48 hrs before          | First night charged             |
| Non-refundable      | Any time                  | No refund                       |
| No-show             | —                         | Full booking amount forfeited   |

- EU consumer rights: Clear cancellation terms must be communicated at booking
- UK: Package holiday bookings have additional protections under Package Travel Regulations

### Middle East & International
- Luxury properties: Non-refundable rates heavily discounted; flexible rates at 20–40% premium
- UAE / Qatar: 24–48 hr cancellation window common; no-show = full night charge
- Holiday packages: Typically non-refundable within 14–30 days of arrival

## Taxes & Fees

### India
- GST @ 12%: Rooms priced ₹1,000–₹7,499/night
- GST @ 18%: Rooms priced ₹7,500+/night
- Corporate bookings: Provide GSTIN for B2B GST credit

### USA
- Hotel taxes vary by city/state: typically 12–20% of room rate
- Resort fees / destination fees: $20–$60/night (mandatory, often not shown in base price)
- Occupancy tax: Charged on top of room rate; varies by municipality
- Examples:
  - New York City: ~14.75% hotel tax + $3.50/night occupancy fee
  - Las Vegas: ~13% hotel tax + resort fees ($35–$50/night)
  - Miami Beach: ~13% hotel tax + resort fees

### Europe
- VAT / TVA / MwSt: 5–25% depending on country
  - UK: 20% VAT on hotel rooms
  - France: 10% TVA on accommodation
  - Germany: 7% VAT (reduced rate for accommodation)
- City / Tourist Tax: Often separate and paid in cash at hotel
  - Paris: €0.25–€5/person/night depending on star rating
  - Rome: €3–€7/person/night
  - Amsterdam: 12.5% of room rate (city tax)
  - Barcelona: €0.75–€3.50/person/night

### Middle East
- UAE: 10% service charge + 10% tourism fee + 10% municipality fee + 6% city tax (varies by emirate; roughly 30%+ on base rate in Dubai)
- Qatar: 10% service charge; no income/tourism tax for most hotels
- Saudi Arabia: 15% VAT on all hotel stays

### Asia-Pacific
- Singapore: 9% GST + 10% service charge (standard "++ pricing")
- Thailand: 7% VAT + 10% service charge (also "++" pricing)
- Japan: 10% consumption tax; some cities add lodging tax (¥200–¥1,000/person/night)
- Australia: 10% GST included in displayed rates

## Do Not Disturb & Room Service Hours

- **Budget / 3-star (global):** Room service 7 AM–11 PM; limited DND
- **4-star:** Room service 6 AM–midnight; full DND
- **5-star / Luxury:** 24-hour room service; butler service; full DND + privacy management

## Resort & Mandatory Fees (Watch Out For)

- **USA Resort Fees:** $20–$60/night charged regardless of cancellation, not always shown upfront. Common in Las Vegas, Miami, Hawaii, NYC.
- **UAE Service Charge:** 10% service charge on all F&B and room rates; standard across the Gulf.
- **Singapore / Thailand "++":** Prices displayed without service charge or tax; final bill can be 19–20% higher.
- **Europe City Tax:** Collected at check-out; typically €1–€7/person/night; not included in OTA prices.
- **India (Luxury):** GST + restaurant service charge (10%) + resort fees (if applicable); grand total can be 30–40% above base room rate.
