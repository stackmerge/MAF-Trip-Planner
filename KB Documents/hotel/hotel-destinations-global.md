## INDIA

### Business Travel Cities

#### Hyderabad
- Best areas: HITEC City (IT hub), Banjara Hills (upscale), Secunderabad (central), Gachibowli (corporate)
- Business hotels: Novotel HICC, Hyatt Hyderabad Gachibowli, ITC Kohenur, Marriott Hyderabad
- Leisure add-on: Charminar, Golconda Fort, Ramoji Film City

#### Bengaluru
- Best areas: MG Road (central), Whitefield (IT), Indiranagar (trendy), Koramangala (startup hub)
- Business hotels: The Leela Palace Bengaluru, ITC Gardenia, Marriott Whitefield

#### Delhi NCR
- Best areas: Aerocity (airport), Connaught Place (central), Gurugram (corporate), Noida (IT)
- Business hotels: Leela Palace Chanakyapuri, ITC Maurya, Marriott Aerocity, Hyatt Regency Delhi

#### Mumbai
- Best areas: BKC (corporate), Andheri (airport/BPO), Colaba (heritage/tourism), Powai (IT)
- Hotels: Taj Mahal Palace, ITC Grand Central, Hyatt Regency Andheri, Ibis Mumbai Airport

### Leisure Destinations — India

#### Goa
- North Goa: Lively, budget-to-mid, party scene | Price: ₹1,500–₹12,000/night
- South Goa: Quiet, luxury resorts, pristine beaches | Price: ₹6,000–₹65,000/night

#### Rajasthan
- Jaipur: Palace hotels, Pink City; ₹3,000–₹40,000/night
- Udaipur: Romantic lake-view stays; ₹5,000–₹60,000/night
- Jodhpur / Jaisalmer: Heritage forts, desert camps; ₹4,000–₹1,00,000/night

#### Kerala
- Alleppey: Houseboat stays; ₹5,000–₹25,000/night
- Munnar: Tea estate stays; ₹3,000–₹12,000/night
- Kovalam / Varkala: Beach resorts; ₹3,500–₹20,000/night

#### Hill Stations
- Shimla / Manali: Colonial properties, river-view resorts; ₹2,000–₹15,000/night
- Ooty / Coorg: Estate stays, eco-resorts; ₹2,500–₹18,000/night

## USA

### Business Travel Cities

#### New York City (NYC)
- Best areas: Midtown Manhattan (business/Times Square), Lower Manhattan (financial district), Brooklyn (trendy/budget)
- Business hotels: The Peninsula, Park Hyatt, Conrad NYC, Marriott Marquis
- Mid-range: Courtyard by Marriott Midtown, Hampton Inn Times Square
- Budget: Pod 51, citizenM, ibis Styles
- Avg price: $150–$250/night (budget) | $300–$600/night (mid) | $600–$2,000+/night (luxury)
- Caution: Mandatory NYC hotel taxes ~14.75% + occupancy surcharges

#### Los Angeles (LA)
- Best areas: Beverly Hills (luxury), Santa Monica (beach), Downtown (business), Hollywood (tourism)
- Business hotels: The Beverly Hills Hotel, InterContinental DTLA, JW Marriott LA Live
- Mid-range: Courtyard Century City, Residence Inn Downtown
- Avg price: $120–$200/night (budget) | $250–$500/night (mid) | $500–$2,500+/night (luxury)

#### Chicago
- Best areas: Loop (downtown), River North (nightlife), Magnificent Mile (shopping/tourism)
- Business hotels: The Langham, Fairmont Chicago, Marriott Magnificent Mile
- Mid-range: Hyatt Place Chicago, Hampton Inn Downtown
- Avg price: $100–$180/night (budget) | $200–$400/night (mid) | $400–$1,000+/night (luxury)

#### Las Vegas
- Strip hotels: Bellagio, MGM Grand, Caesars Palace, Wynn, The Venetian, Aria, Vdara
- Off-Strip: Palms, The Strat, Rio (budget)
- Note: Room rates are artificially low ($40–$150); resort fees ($35–$50/night) are mandatory
- Avg price (all-in): $80–$150/night (off-peak, off-strip) | $150–$400/night (mid, Strip) | $300–$1,500+/night (luxury, weekend)

### Leisure Destinations — USA

#### Hawaii (Maui, Oahu, Big Island, Kauai)
- Waikiki Beach (Oahu): Busy tourist area; Moana Surfrider, Hilton Hawaiian Village
- Maui: Luxury resorts in Wailea; Grand Wailea, Four Seasons Maui
- Avg price: $200–$400/night (mid) | $500–$1,500+/night (luxury)
- Note: 18% transient accommodations tax in Hawaii

#### Miami & Miami Beach
- South Beach: Art Deco architecture; The Betsy, Faena Hotel, The Setai
- Brickell: Business district; JW Marriott, Four Seasons
- Avg price: $150–$250/night (budget) | $300–$600/night (mid) | $500–$2,000+/night (luxury)
- Note: Heavy resort fees; book early for peak season (Dec–Apr)

## EUROPE

### Business Travel Cities

#### London, UK
- Best areas: Mayfair/Knightsbridge (luxury), City of London (financial), South Bank (mid), Heathrow area (airport)
- Luxury: The Savoy, Claridge's, The Ritz, Four Seasons Park Lane
- Mid-range: Marriott County Hall, Holiday Inn Kensington, Park Plaza Victoria
- Budget: Premier Inn, Travelodge, ibis London
- Avg price: £80–£120/night (budget) | £150–£300/night (mid) | £350–£2,000+/night (luxury)

#### Paris, France
- Best areas: 1st–8th arrondissements (central); Saint-Germain (boutique); La Défense (business)
- Luxury: The Ritz Paris, Hotel Plaza Athénée, Le Bristol
- Mid-range: Pullman Paris Montparnasse, Novotel Paris Centre
- Budget: ibis Paris, Generator Hostel
- Avg price: €80–€130/night (budget) | €180–€350/night (mid) | €500–€5,000+/night (luxury)
- Note: City tax €1–€5/person/night; 10% VAT included

#### Amsterdam, Netherlands
- Best areas: Canal Ring (central/tourism), Museumplein (culture), Zuidas (business)
- Luxury: Waldorf Astoria Amsterdam, Sofitel Legend The Grand
- Mid-range: Marriott Amsterdam, NH Collection Doelen
- Budget: ibis Amsterdam, StayOkay Hostel
- Avg price: €90–€150/night (budget) | €200–€400/night (mid) | €450–€1,500+/night (luxury)
- Note: 12.5% city tax added at checkout

#### Barcelona, Spain
- Best areas: Gothic Quarter (tourism), Eixample (design/business), Barceloneta (beach)
- Luxury: Hotel Arts, Mandarin Oriental Barcelona, W Barcelona
- Mid-range: Meliá Barcelona Sky, NH Collection Gran Hotel Calderón
- Budget: Motel One, Generator Barcelona, ibis Ramblas
- Avg price: €60–€110/night (budget) | €150–€300/night (mid) | €350–€1,200+/night (luxury)

### Leisure Destinations — Europe

#### Santorini, Greece
- Areas: Oia (sunset views), Fira (restaurants/nightlife), Imerovigli (quieter luxury)
- Luxury: Grace Santorini, Canaves Oia, Katikies
- Mid-range: Santorini Princess, Vedema Resort
- Avg price: €120–€250/night (mid) | €400–€2,000+/night (luxury caldera-view)
- Peak: June–September; book 6–12 months ahead for cave suites

#### Amalfi Coast & Positano, Italy
- Iconic areas: Positano, Ravello, Amalfi town
- Luxury: Le Sirenuse, Palazzo Avino, Belmond Hotel Caruso
- Mid-range: Hotel Savoia, Casa Maresca
- Avg price: €150–€300/night (mid) | €600–€3,000+/night (luxury cliffside)

#### Swiss Alps (Zermatt, St. Moritz, Interlaken)
- Luxury: The Omnia Zermatt, Badrutt's Palace (St. Moritz), Victoria-Jungfrau (Interlaken)
- Mid-range: Chalet-style hotels; ₹15,000–₹40,000/night equivalent (CHF 150–400)
- Peak: December–March (ski season) and July–August (hiking)

## MIDDLE EAST

### Dubai, UAE
- Best areas: Downtown Dubai (Burj Khalifa), Dubai Marina/JBR (beach), DIFC (business), Deira (budget)
- Ultra-luxury: Burj Al Arab, Atlantis The Palm, Four Seasons DIFC, Jumeirah Al Naseem
- Luxury: Address Downtown, JW Marriott Marquis, W Dubai
- Mid-range: Hyatt Place Dubai, Pullman Dubai, ibis Dubai
- Budget: Premier Inn Dubai, OYO Dubai
- Avg price: AED 150–350/night (budget) | AED 400–900/night (mid) | AED 900–10,000+/night (luxury)

### Abu Dhabi, UAE
- Best areas: Corniche (luxury), Yas Island (entertainment), Al Maryah Island (business)
- Luxury: Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental, Four Seasons Abu Dhabi, Louvre Abu Dhabi (adjacent)

## ASIA-PACIFIC

### Singapore
- Best areas: Marina Bay (luxury/business), Orchard (mid/shopping), Little India/Chinatown (budget), Sentosa (resort)
- Luxury: Marina Bay Sands, Raffles Hotel, Capella Singapore, Four Seasons
- Mid-range: Andaz Singapore, Swissôtel The Stamford, Parkroyal Collection
- Budget: ibis Singapore, The Pod, Adler Luxury Hostel
- Avg price: SGD 80–150/night (budget) | SGD 200–400/night (mid) | SGD 400–1,500+/night (luxury)
- Note: 9% GST + 10% service charge = ~19–20% on top of listed prices

### Bangkok, Thailand
- Best areas: Sukhumvit (expat/tourist), Silom (business), Riverside (luxury), Khao San (backpacker)
- Luxury: Mandarin Oriental Bangkok, Capella Bangkok, Four Seasons Chao Phraya
- Mid-range: Sofitel Bangkok, Mercure Sukhumvit, Novotel Siam
- Budget: ibis Bangkok, The Yard Hostel, NapPark Hostel
- Avg price: THB 800–1,500/night (budget) | THB 2,500–6,000/night (mid) | THB 6,000–30,000+/night (luxury)

### Bali, Indonesia
- Best areas: Seminyak (upscale), Ubud (wellness/nature), Nusa Dua (resort beach), Canggu (digital nomad/surf)
- Luxury: Four Seasons Sayan (Ubud), Ayana Resort (Jimbaran), COMO Uma Ubud
- Mid-range: Alaya Resort Ubud, The Layar Seminyak
- Budget: Guesthouses and villas via Airbnb; many private pool villas at THB/IDR budget rates
- Avg price: IDR 300,000–800,000/night (~₹1,500–₹4,000) (budget) | IDR 2,000,000–8,000,000/night (mid-luxury)

### Tokyo, Japan
- Best areas: Shinjuku (business/entertainment), Ginza (luxury), Asakusa (tourism), Shibuya (youth culture), Marunouchi (business)
- Luxury: The Tokyo Edition Ginza, Park Hyatt Tokyo, Aman Tokyo, Mandarin Oriental Tokyo
- Mid-range: Hyatt Regency Tokyo, Cerulean Tower, Keio Plaza Hotel
- Budget: Dormy Inn, APA Hotels, business hotels (¥6,000–¥12,000/night)
- Avg price: ¥6,000–¥12,000/night (budget) | ¥20,000–¥50,000/night (mid) | ¥60,000–¥200,000+/night (luxury)
- Note: 10% consumption tax + lodging tax ¥200–¥1,000/person/night

### Maldives
- Structure: Each resort is on its own private island; speedboat or seaplane transfer required
- Luxury: One&Only Reethi Rah, Soneva Fushi, Six Senses Laamu, Gili Lankanfushi
- Mid-range: Oblu Select, Sun Siyam Iru Fushi, Sandies Bathala
- Budget: Guesthouses on local islands (Maafushi, Dhigurah); $60–$150/night including meals
- Avg price: $200–$400/night (guesthouse/mid) | $800–$5,000+/night (luxury overwater villa)
- Note: Transfer costs ($100–$600/person via seaplane) are significant; factor into budget
