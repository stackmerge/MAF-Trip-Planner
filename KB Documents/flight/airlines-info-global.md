# Airlines & Route Information — Global

## Indian Domestic Airlines

### IndiGo (6E)
- Type: Low-cost carrier
- Hub: Delhi (DEL), Mumbai (BOM)
- Fleet: Airbus A320, A321neo
- USP: Largest domestic network in India, strong on-time performance
- Baggage: 7kg carry-on; checked bag paid add-on (15kg = ₹599)
- Meals: Buy on board
- Customer Care: 0124-6173838

### Air India (AI)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Delhi (DEL), Mumbai (BOM)
- Fleet: Boeing 787, 777, Airbus A320
- USP: Only Indian carrier with wide-body international fleet
- Baggage: 15kg (Economy domestic), 25kg (Economy international)
- Meals: Complimentary on all flights
- Customer Care: 1860-233-1407

### Vistara (UK)
- Note: Merged with Air India as of 2024. Existing bookings honored under Air India.

### SpiceJet (SG)
- Type: Low-cost carrier
- Hub: Delhi (DEL)
- Fleet: Boeing 737, Bombardier Q400
- USP: Good tier-2 city connectivity
- Baggage: 7kg carry-on; 15kg checked (paid)
- Customer Care: 0124-7100888

### Akasa Air (QP)
- Type: Low-cost carrier
- Hub: Mumbai (BOM), Delhi (DEL)
- Fleet: Boeing 737 MAX
- USP: Newest Indian airline; modern fleet; competitive pricing
- Baggage: 7kg carry-on; 15kg checked (paid, ₹499 onwards)
- Customer Care: 080-68990000

## US Domestic Airlines

### Delta Air Lines (DL)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Atlanta (ATL), New York JFK, Minneapolis (MSP), Salt Lake City (SLC)
- Fleet: Boeing 737, 757, 767, 777, Airbus A220, A319, A320, A330, A350
- USP: Largest airline by fleet size; strong premium product; extensive SkyMiles loyalty program
- Baggage: 1 carry-on + personal item; first checked bag $35
- Meals: Complimentary in First/Business; snacks in Economy
- Frequent Flyer: SkyMiles

### United Airlines (UA)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Chicago O'Hare (ORD), Houston (IAH), Newark (EWR), San Francisco (SFO), Denver (DEN)
- Fleet: Boeing 737, 757, 767, 777, 787; Airbus A319, A320
- USP: Largest international route network from the US
- Baggage: 1 carry-on + personal item; first checked bag $35
- Frequent Flyer: MileagePlus

### American Airlines (AA)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Dallas/Fort Worth (DFW), Miami (MIA), Charlotte (CLT), Philadelphia (PHL)
- Fleet: Boeing 737, 777, 787; Airbus A319, A320, A321
- USP: World's largest airline by number of passengers
- Baggage: 1 carry-on + personal item; first checked bag $35
- Frequent Flyer: AAdvantage

### Southwest Airlines (WN)
- Type: Low-cost carrier
- Hub: Dallas Love Field (DAL), Chicago Midway (MDW), Baltimore (BWI)
- Fleet: Boeing 737
- USP: No change/cancellation fees; 2 free checked bags for all passengers
- Baggage: 2 free checked bags; no seat assignments
- Frequent Flyer: Rapid Rewards

### JetBlue Airways (B6)
- Type: Low-cost carrier (premium LCC)
- Hub: New York JFK, Boston (BOS), Fort Lauderdale (FLL)
- Fleet: Airbus A220, A320, A321
- USP: Transatlantic routes; Mint business class; free WiFi and seatback TVs
- Baggage: 1 carry-on + personal item; first checked bag $35 (base fare)
- Frequent Flyer: TrueBlue

## European Airlines

### Ryanair (FR)
- Type: Ultra-low-cost carrier
- Hub: Dublin (DUB), London Stansted (STN)
- Fleet: Boeing 737
- USP: Cheapest fares in Europe; extensive pan-European network
- Baggage: 1 small personal bag free; larger cabin bag £/€6–35 add-on; checked bag paid
- Customer Care: Online-only primary; limited phone support
- Note: Airport check-in = €55; web check-in mandatory

### EasyJet (U2)
- Type: Low-cost carrier
- Hub: London Gatwick (LGW), London Luton (LTN), Amsterdam (AMS), Berlin (BER)
- Fleet: Airbus A319, A320, A321
- USP: Punctual; major EU city connectivity; allocated seating
- Baggage: Small personal bag free; cabin bag add-on; 23kg checked bag paid
- Frequent Flyer: Flight Club

### Wizz Air (W6)
- Type: Ultra-low-cost carrier
- Hub: Budapest (BUD), Warsaw (WAW), Bucharest (OTP)
- Fleet: Airbus A320, A321
- USP: Eastern Europe specialist; very low base fares; Wizz Discount Club
- Baggage: Very restrictive; most bags are paid add-ons
- Note: Wizz Priority or Wizz Go bundle recommended for cabin bag inclusion

### Lufthansa (LH)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Frankfurt (FRA), Munich (MUC)
- Fleet: Airbus A319–A380, Boeing 747, 777, 787
- USP: Premium German carrier; strong Star Alliance network
- Baggage: 1 × 23kg checked bag included in Economy (on most fares)
- Meals: Complimentary on medium/long-haul; buy-on-board short-haul
- Frequent Flyer: Miles & More (Star Alliance)

### British Airways (BA)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: London Heathrow (LHR), London Gatwick (LGW)
- Fleet: Boeing 777, 787, Airbus A319, A320, A321, A380
- USP: Strong transatlantic network; Oneworld alliance; Club World business class
- Baggage: 1 × 23kg included in Economy on most intercontinental routes
- Frequent Flyer: Executive Club (Avios)

### Air France (AF)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG)
- Fleet: Airbus A318–A380, Boeing 777, 787
- USP: SkyTeam hub; La Première first class; Paris connectivity
- Frequent Flyer: Flying Blue (also covers KLM)

### KLM Royal Dutch Airlines (KL)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS)
- Fleet: Boeing 737, 777, 787; Airbus A330
- USP: Strong Africa and Caribbean networks; excellent lounge at AMS
- Frequent Flyer: Flying Blue

## Middle East Airlines

### Emirates (EK)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Dubai (DXB)
- Fleet: Airbus A380, Boeing 777
- USP: World's largest A380 operator; ICE entertainment system; global reach via DXB hub
- Baggage (Economy): 25–35kg (route-dependent)
- From India: DEL, BOM, HYD, BLR, MAA, CCU, AMD, COK
- Frequent Flyer: Skywards

### Qatar Airways (QR)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Doha Hamad International (DOH)
- Fleet: Airbus A320, A330, A350, A380, Boeing 777, 787
- USP: Multiple Skytrax Airline of the Year winner; QSuite business class; Oneworld
- Baggage (Economy): 23–30kg
- From India: DEL, BOM, HYD, BLR, MAA, CCU
- Frequent Flyer: Privilege Club

### Etihad Airways (EY)

- Hub: Abu Dhabi (AUH)

## Asia-Pacific Airlines

### Singapore Airlines (SQ)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Singapore Changi (SIN)
- Fleet: Airbus A350, A380, Boeing 777, 787
- USP: Consistently ranked world's best airline; Singapore Girl service; Star Alliance
- Baggage (Economy): 25–30kg
- From India: DEL, BOM, HYD, BLR, MAA, CCU, AMD
- Frequent Flyer: KrisFlyer

### Cathay Pacific (CX)
- Type: Full-service carrier
- Hub: Hong Kong (HKG)
- Fleet: Airbus A330, A350, Boeing 777
- USP: Award-winning business class (Aria Suite); strong Asia-Pacific network; Oneworld
- Baggage (Economy): 23–30kg
- Frequent Flyer: Asia Miles

### AirAsia (AK / FD / QZ)
- Type: Ultra-low-cost carrier
- Hub: Kuala Lumpur KLIA2 (KUL)
- Fleet: Airbus A320, A321
- USP: Cheapest fares in Southeast Asia; extensive regional network
- Baggage: Very restrictive; all bags paid add-ons

### Japan Airlines (JL)

### Korean Air (KE)

- Hub: Seoul Incheon (ICN)
