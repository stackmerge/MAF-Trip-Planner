## Cancellation & Refund Policies

### India
- Within 24 hours of booking (departure 7+ days away): Full refund
- 7+ days before departure: Refund minus ₹1,000–₹2,500 cancellation fee
- 3–6 days before departure: 50% refund
- Less than 3 days: No refund; travel credit may apply
- DGCA mandate: Airlines must refund within 7 days for airline-initiated cancellations

### USA
- DOT Rule: Full refund within 24 hours of booking (departure 7+ days away)
- Basic Economy: Generally non-refundable; no changes allowed
- Standard/Flex fares: Change fees waived by most major carriers post-COVID (permanent policy at Delta, United, American)
- Airline-initiated cancellations: Full refund required by DOT regulations

### Europe (EU261/2004 Regulation)
- Cancelled flights with <14 days notice: Compensation €250–€600 based on distance
  - <1,500km: €250
  - 1,500–3,500km: €400
  - >3,500km: €600
- Delays >3 hours (passenger's fault of airline): Same compensation scale
- Refund or re-routing must be offered within 7 days

### Middle East / International (General)
- Most carriers: Non-refundable base fares; taxes refundable
- Flex/Business fares: Full or partial refund minus fee
- Timeline: 7–21 business days depending on carrier and payment method

## Important Travel Regulations — Global

### India
- Valid photo ID mandatory: Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, PAN Card
- DigiYatra: Paperless facial recognition boarding at DEL, BOM, HYD, BLR, MAA, CCU
- DGCA passenger rights: Denied boarding compensation ₹5,000–₹10,000

### USA
- TSA PreCheck: Expedited security screening; $85 for 5 years
- Global Entry: Expedited customs re-entry; $100 for 5 years (includes TSA PreCheck)
- REAL ID: Required for domestic flights from May 7, 2025 — must be REAL ID-compliant state ID or passport
- CBP One App: Required for certain international entry processes

### Europe (Schengen Area)
- ETIAS (European Travel Information and Authorization System): Launching 2025; €7 fee for non-EU travelers to Schengen countries
- EU passenger rights under EU261/2004 apply to all flights departing EU airports
- Passport validity: Must be valid for 3+ months beyond travel dates for Schengen visits

### UAE / Middle East
- UAE: Visit visa on arrival for many nationalities; evisa for others
- Saudi Arabia: eVisa required; available online
- Qatar: Hayya card / visa on arrival for many nationalities

### Australia / New Zealand
- ETA (Electronic Travel Authority): AUD $20 for eligible passport holders
- NZeTA: NZD $17 for visa-waiver countries + $35 International Visitor Conservation and Tourism Levy

### USA Entry (ESTA)
- Visa Waiver Program: ESTA authorization required; $21 fee; valid 2 years
- Required for 42 VWP-eligible countries; must be applied 72+ hours before travel
